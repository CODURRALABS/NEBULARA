NEBULARA UNIVERSAL DISTRIBUTION
==============================

pkg/
├── runtime/     # Portable executable (no install)
│   ├── nebulara.exe     # x64 PE native
│   ├── libffi.dll       # FFI adapters
│   └── nvulkan.dll      # GPU acceleration
│
├── sdk/         # Development tools
│   ├── compiler/        # .nbs -> bytecode
│   ├── headers/         # nebulara.h
│   └── lib/             # libnebulara.a
│
└── ffi/         # Universal language bridge
    ├── nodejs.dll
    ├── python.dll
    ├── java.dll
    └── cpp.dll
